Problem Statement:
Developers usually are unable to have a centralized place to present their skills, projects, and more information about themselves outside of a standard resume. The point of me creating this website was to show employers that you I have knowledge on frontend and backend while giving the chance to contact me directly for opportunities. 

My Objectives with this project were the following:
1. Build a multi-page portfolio site that has all my skills, projects, research, education, and any other background in one singular place
2. Show my frontend and backend knowledge through the use of Javascript, Firebase, and Google Cloud
3. Give employers and visitors the ability to contact me directly and include a community blog that allows users to write their thoughts.
